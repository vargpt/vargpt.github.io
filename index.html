Hi, I'm Varun and this is my academic website. I am a graduate student in the Computer Science department at the University of Pennsylvania, where I have the pleasure of being advised by Michael Kearns and Aaron Roth.

My research interests are in machine learning and algorithmic game theory. I am motivated by improving considerations of fairness, privacy, and robustness in practical systems. I am also interested in technology policy and in particular, operationalizing legal concepts in this sphere by making them technically rigorous.

I was fortunate to start research early as a student thanks to support by all my mentors. I enjoy helping students learn about research, so please get in touch if you want to discuss my work!
