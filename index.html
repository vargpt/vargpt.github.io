Varun Gupta

Hello! I am a graduate student in the Computer Science department at the University of Pennsylvania, where I am fortunate to be advised by Michael Kearns and Aaron Roth. Previously, I was an undergrad at Georgia Tech where I studied Computer Science and Public Policy.

My research interests are in machine learning and algorithmic game theory. My work focuses on sequential online prediction and responsible data-driven decision-making.

I enjoy talking with students about research so please get in touch if you want to discuss my work!
